Route France

Day 1: Paris

Visit the Eiffel Tower and Champs Elysees, explore the Louvre and the Marais, and dine at a Parisian restaurant and walk along the Seine.

Day 2: Normandy

Travel to Normandy, visit the D-Day beaches and the American Cemetery, and dine in a coastal town.

Day 3: Loire Valley

Travel to the Loire Valley, explore a castle, such as Château de Chambord or Château de Chenonceau, and Enjoy local cuisine.

Day 4: Provence

Trip to the Provence region explore lavender fields and visit a local market and dine at a traditional Provençal restaurant.

Day 5: Blue Coast

Travel to the Côte d'Azur, discover Nice and stroll along the Promenade des Anglais and finally have dinner in front of the sea.

Route Cruise

Marsella

Embark in Marseille, the vibrant port city. Enjoy the mix of cultures and flavors in the Vieux-Port and discover the Basilica of Notre-Dame de la Garde for panoramic views.

Niza y Mónaco

Sail to Nice, where Mediterranean charm meets elegance. Explore the Promenade des Anglais and then visit Monaco to experience luxury at the famous casino and the Prince's Palace.

Lyon and Beaujolais

Delve into the Beaujolais wine region from Lyon. Enjoy wine tastings and explore the vineyard-covered hills before returning to Lyon to explore the city of gastronomy.

Estrasburgo

Cruise along the Rhine River to Strasbourg, a jewel in the Alsace region. Discover the cathedral Gothic style, stroll along the enchanting canals and sample local delicacies.

Paris

Conclude your trip in the city of lights, Paris. Explore iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and stroll along the banks of the Seine. A unique cruise that combines the beauty of the Mediterranean, the wine exquisiteness and Parisian charm in 5 unforgettable days.¡Bon voyage!
